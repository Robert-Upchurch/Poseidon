Your path to sea

From application to first contract.

Four steps. We walk with you through each one.

1

Apply

Complete our 10-minute online intake. Tell us about your background, your preferred role, and your availability. You will receive a tracking link by email so you can check progress any time.

2

Documents

We confirm what is required for your specific role and route: STCW Basic Safety, seaman's book, ENG-1 or PEME medical, valid passport, C1/D visa for US-routed ships, and any role-specific certifications (Marlins for hospitality, for example). We will guide you to recognized providers if you do not have these yet.

3

Interview

Once documents are on track, we introduce you to the cruise lines that match your profile. Most interviews happen by video. You may interview with more than one line in parallel.

4

Placement

Contract offer in hand, we coordinate your join date, travel to port, and onboard induction. Typical contracts run 6 to 9 months at sea, with paid breaks between contracts.
