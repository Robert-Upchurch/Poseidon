Tax

OBBBA — P.L. 119-21 (signed July 4, 2025)

  • $40K SALT cap (was $10K) — high-tax-state impact for non-FL income.
  • Mortgage interest cap retained at $750K acquisition indebtedness.
  • Section 174 R&D restoration — full expensing of domestic R&D restored retroactive to 2022; foreign R&D still amortized 15 years.
  • 100% bonus depreciation permanent (no phase-down).
  • QBI 20% deduction made permanent.
  • Section 179 cap raised to $2.56M with $3.13M phase-out threshold.
  • NOL strategy: 80% taxable-income limitation retained; carryforward indefinite.

Source: lifted from Upchurch Financial Dashboard v4.7.36 §#tax.

Florida — state context

  • No personal income tax.
  • Corporate income tax 5.5% (with $50K exemption).
  • Sales tax 6% state + 0.5–2% county discretionary surtax.
  • Right-to-work state — applies to CTI employment matters.
